# AI Legal Dost ⚖️
**Legal Futurism Meets Indian Simplicity.**

AI Legal Dost is an AI-powered legal assistant designed for the Indian landscape, developed by **NetWhy Studios**. 

### Core Pillars:
- **Simplified Parser:** Translates legalese into plain English/Hinglish.
- **Document Genie:** Helps in drafting FIRs, RTIs, and contracts.
- **Legal Lens:** OCR-powered analysis of legal notices.
- **Compliance Health Check:** For citizens and MSMEs.
